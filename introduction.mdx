**We are an interdisciplinary research group devoted to creating AI-based solutions for healthcare. We specialize in developing cutting-edge artificial intelligence technologies to revolutionize the medical field, with a focus on open-source projects, education, consulting, and commercial solutions**

<Card title="Open-source projects" icon="bolt">
  At the heart of our business are open-source projects that, among other things, support the work of doctors in Pomerania. We have created AI models for parameterization of radiological reports and prediction of the course of Covid-19 based on blood counts. We also create tools for collecting and analyzing medical data and for creating bots based on language models.
</Card>
